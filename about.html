About Us

Marpho is an authorized Howo/Sinotruk dealer in Tanzania, importing trucks directly from China to ensure authenticity and high quality. The company supplies all major truck types, including tractor heads, dump trucks, concrete mixers, and trailers, to meet diverse transport needs. These trucks are suitable for industries such as construction, logistics, mining, and infrastructure development. By offering multiple truck categories in one place, Marpho provides flexible and reliable transport solutions for different operations. This wide selection allows customers to choose vehicles that match their workload, terrain, and business requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

→ Which countries do you import trucks from?
We import high-quality trucks from trusted international markets such as China .All vehicles are carefully inspected to ensure they meet our quality and performance standards before shipping.
→ Do you handle shipping and customs clearance?
Yes. We manage the entire shipping process, including international transport, customs clearance, and port handling. This ensures a smooth and hassle-free experience for our customers.
→ How long does delivery take after purchase?
Delivery time depends on the country of origin and shipping schedules. On average, it takes 4–8 weeks from confirmation of purchase until the truck arrives at the destination port.
→ Are the trucks inspected before shipping?
Absolutely. Each truck undergoes a thorough inspection to verify its mechanical condition, mileage, and overall quality. Inspection reports can be provided upon request.
→ What documents will I receive with the truck?
You will receive all necessary documents, including the bill of lading, export certificate, invoice, and any required import or registration documents to help you register the truck locally.
