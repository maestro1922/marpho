Welcome to Marpho

Marpho is a company that specializes in the importation and sale of trucks from international markets. It sources heavy-duty and medium-duty trucks from China, ensuring high quality and performance. The company manages logistics, customs clearance, and documentation to deliver vehicles efficiently to local buyers. By offering reliable, globally sourced trucks, Morpho provides transport operators with options not easily available domestically. This supports the growth of the regional transportation industry by expanding access to diverse and durable truck models.
